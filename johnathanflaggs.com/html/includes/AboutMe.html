About Me

"[...] For dreams too easily become the wishes of men too tired to imagine and achieve."


Let's talk software! I'm a full-on software enthusiast with an addiction to learning. I graduated from the University of California, Riverside with a Bachelor's Degree in Mechanical Engineering and a concentration in Control Theory. During my years at UCR and UCD, I sort of veered from my academic background and became facinated with Embedded Controls. Once I discovered the power of mechatronics I never looked back.

I pursued a career in Software Development and have been very fortunate to work with some great companies and gain experience working on some very complex and heavy-duty projects. I've helped companies develop ground-up applications using platforms such as RS-Logix, TwinCAT 2 and 3, C# .NET, Fanuc TP, Cognex, Baumer, and a few other controls platforms.

Along the way, I discovered my passion for business and Fin-Tech. I've dabbled in running a small business called 10-Engineers and I also enjoy developing Quantitative Finance / Algorithmic Trading strategies using platforms like NinjaTrader 8 and TradeStation 10.

I've recently begun developing a standalone algorithmic trading platform called J-LAB using broker API's for charting data and order routing -- just to give me more flexibility and control over my trading.
